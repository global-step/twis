Employment

If you are interested in working at our school, we encourage you to continue to check this webpage regularly; new vacancies are posted as they become available. Open CV’s can be sent to:


185 Umetsubo-machi, Hachioji city, Tokyo, Japan 192-0013
admin@tokyowest.jp


Please see below for current job postings. Please note: At TWIS we receive a lot of applications each year for a limited number of positions. We apologize that we are not able to respond to each applicant individually and will only contact those whose applications we wish to pursue further.


Health and Physical Education Teacher

We are seeking an experienced Health and Physical Education teacher for grades K3 up to Grade 9.
The successful applicant will have experience in designing and teaching Health and Physical Education classes in an International School environment, an ability to implement a student centred classroom and design interesting and challenging units.
This position will also include the implementation of the annual "Sports Day" , coaching and running an after school program, and implementation and creation of extra-curricular events.
Minimum of Bachelor in Education, teachers licence and 2 years experience.


Science teacher

We are seeking an experienced Health and Physical Education teacher for grades K3 up to Grade 9.
We are seeking an experienced science teacher who can teach Middle school science, science labs from Grade 1 - Grade 9, and who would over see the school wide science program.
Minimum of Bachelor in Education, teachers licence and 2 years experience.


Homeroom teacher

We are seeking an elementary homeroom teacher,
The successful applicant will have an ability to facilitate a student centred classroom. Experience in backward design unit planning, differentiation for ESL learners and digital learning is desired.
All teachers are required to contribute to extra curricular activities of the school.
Minimum of Bachelor in Education, teachers licence and 2 years experience
